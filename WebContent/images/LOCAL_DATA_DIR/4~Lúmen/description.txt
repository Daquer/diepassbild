LÚMEN (2015).<br/><br/>a photograph series for a t-shirt collection inspired by the animals who live in the deep seas. "lumen", in honor of the effects that the surface and the depths lights cause in the water. all the t-shirt prints were made from collages of fish-rays and water photograms in ilford paper.<br/><br/>cameras: polaroid supercolor 635CL / yashica 108 multiprogram.<br/><br/>films: impossible project color instant film / ilford hp5.<br/><br/>location: arpoador, rj, br.
